Cake boxes and combo boxes

We also provide cake boxes and combo boxes for taste testing. The cake box will include different kinds of sponges and frostings, so you can make any combination you want for your dream cake. The combo box will include generally one of each type of dessert (some of them will be more than 1 because they vary in flavour). To book a cake or combo box you can get in touch with us by calling because they are made especially for the boxes. See prices below.
