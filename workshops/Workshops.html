Workshops

Because we love desserts and we enjoy seeing people love them we thought that creating workshops would be a great idea. People can come, learn how to make some basic recipes and make them at home so other people can enjoy them too.

You’ll be able to come and learn a few tricks from us and then leave with a care package that contains the recipes and the ingredients you need for the desserts you made with us. It’s a lovely time you can enjoy with friends, family and, why not, even strangers.
